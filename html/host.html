What hosting entails

In order to reach as many possible students as possible, there will be many different Politics Rewired Hackathons in many different locations, each building off of all the previous ones. Anybody can host a Politics Rewired event, and we rely on you taking the initiative to get one started in your area to make them happen.

In order to host a Politics Rewired Hackathon, you will need:
  • rooms full of tables and chairs, good Internet access, and bathrooms
  • enough food and drink to feed people attending for the duration of the event

We will help:
  • find partners and donors, preferably local, that will help pay for the costs of renting the space, food, and Internet if necessary
  • advertise the event and help people make travel arrangements to come
  • develop programming for your event to make it fun, interesting, and productive.
