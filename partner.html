Why become a partner

     Since the attendees of Politics Rewired events will largely be young people who have less experience in organizing or activism, it makes sense to take guidance from people who will use the output of their work and know what is needed. Politics Rewired partners are in a unique position guide development and request open source features or help setting up existing tools for their organizations.

What do we want in a partner?

     It won’t make sense for all organizations to financially help out with the hackathon – many organizations are strapped for cash and already forced to prioritize.

     Instead, we want local partners that have deep ties to their communities and real problems that need solving. Partnering with a hackathon near you will make it easier for you to show up, talk about how your organization functions, and in turn make connections with local programmers who develop custom tools or introduce you to existing tools. 

     We want partners who will show up with a range of requests. Come ask someone to make you a new logo, redesign your website, or help you set up a mailing list.

     Check out our hackathon finder to find one near you and fill out the form below to let us know you’re interested.
